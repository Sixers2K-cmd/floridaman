The Best Movies According to Florida Man

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Florida Project (2017)

A heartwarming drama set in the outskirts of Orlando, following the lives of a young girl and her struggling mother living in a budget motel

Florida Straits (1986)

A thriller about a retired sea captain who gets caught up in drug smuggling off the Florida coast

A Florida Enchantment (1914)

A silent film that weaves fantasy and comedy, exploring gender transformation in a Florida setting
